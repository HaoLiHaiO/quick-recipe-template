You need to prepare the dough and then let it rest for 25-40 minutes. If you leave it for too long, it crumbles, otherwise, it get too wet and disintegrates when you fill it.

  • Bao the jiaozi Chinese style (make a small circle in the palm of your hand)
  • Make sure they're not too big.
  • Then fill the jiaozi using a tea spoon
  • Close them carefully from the side to the top.
  • Make some water boil.
  • Boil for 7 minutes. Or 8 if you're not good at wrapping the jiaozi like me...
